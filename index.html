Lachlan Ford
Software Engineer

Lachlan Ford is a Software Engineer building the future of Human Computer Symbiosis, based in Seattle, formerly part of Microsoft in the Mixed Reality organisation.

His main contributions have been to the design and development of open standards such as OpenXR and WebXR, as well as the implementation of such standards in products such as HoloLens, Windows Mixed Reality and Chromium based browsers such as Google Chrome and Microsoft Edge.

Formerly he designed and developed interactive simulations and games at Smart Sparrow; A startup out of UNSW, Australia, intending to create adaptive e-learning experiences for higher education.

He is passionate about building the future of computing technology, leveraging it to live richer and more capable lives, create more and gain deeper understanding of the universe.

His other interests include:
AI, Brain Computer Interfaces, Ethereum and DeFi, indie games, shaders, animation, photography, technical music, rockclimbing, lucid dreaming, philosophy of mind, computer science, and human computer interaction.

Feel free to reach out if you would like to collaborate!
